Cracking the Coding Interview Summary By Yasuo Maidana

The firsts chapters of this book are focused on how interviews work. There you can find answers to what you could want from them? (if you were a recruiter) or what to expect from them? (if you were an applicant).

Additionally, the author gives you some hints about what enterprises could want for some positions. Also, she proposes a methodology to solve problems and use big O notation to identify which parts of your code you should optimize.

I really enjoyed chapter 8. Here, the author talked about what to do while and after an interview. Especially talking about what to do/think after an interview, she gives good advice:

  • think about what do you want for your future? and
  • when do you want to have it or start it?
